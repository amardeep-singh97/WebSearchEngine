Wikipedia:Ignore all rules - Wikipedia Wikipedia:Ignore all rules From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search Wikipedia page explaining the policy that Wikipedia does not have firm rules This page documents an English Wikipedia policy. It describes a widely accepted standard that all editors should normally follow. Changes made to it should reflect consensus. Shortcuts WP:IAR WP:IGNORE w.wiki/i If a rule prevents you from improving or maintaining Wikipedia, ignore it.
