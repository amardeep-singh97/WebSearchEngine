From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a maintenance category, used for maintenance of the Wikipedia project. It is not part of the encyclopedia and contains non-article pages, or groups articles by status rather than subject. Do not include this category in content categories.


Description: This category contains content guidelines related to English Wikipedia administrative pages including but not limited to; talk pages, user pages, Wikipedia namespace, file namespace, and template namespace pages. For article related content see Wikipedia content guidelines.
