Making it easier to search the pages within a category.[edit]

Re https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Category:Selective_serotonin_reuptake_inhibitors&oldid=1029813923 do you think I'm right about something like that being a good idea to be included/transcluded onto category pages? Much like the "External tools" links are included on page history pages? The syntax is little documented - e.g. no mention of it at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:Category#Searching_for_pages_in_categories. (Feel free to move this comment to a WT: page for further discussion.) --50.201.195.170 (talk) 07:33, 22 June 2021 (UTC)

Hello! Upon taking a second look, I can see where that would be a useful tool, especially for heavily-populated categories. I will look for a suitable policy page where I can request community input. In the meantime, I will be happy to self-revert for the time being. Thanks! PohranicniStraze (talk) 18:21, 22 June 2021 (UTC)
I've added a request for comment, including your comment from here, at Wikipedia talk:WikiProject Categories, in case you'd like to comment further there. Thanks, and happy editing! PohranicniStraze (talk) 18:42, 22 June 2021 (UTC)
Appreciate you!--50.201.195.170 (talk) 22:58, 29 June 2021 (UTC)
